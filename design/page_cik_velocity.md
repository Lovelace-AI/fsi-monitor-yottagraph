# CIK Velocity

This is a score that measures both a company's own filings, and its connections to other companies. Here is the data we have to work with:

- current directors
- current officers
- filings submitted by this company
- filings submitted by other companies
- key people are listed as directors/officers at other companies
- company A owns a stake in company B (SC 13D/G)
- A lists B as a subsidiary (EX-21)
- A signs agreement with B (8K 1.01)
- A borrows from B (8-K 2.03)
